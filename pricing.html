Pricing FAQ

Is the free tier really free forever?
Yes. The register is public EU data — reading it via the API or the open dataset is free and keyless, with fair-use limits. We only charge for the layer on top: push notifications, guarantees and evidence.
What exactly am I paying for, if the data is free?
Convenience and guarantees you can't get from the raw EU page: webhooks so you're notified the moment the register changes (instead of polling), a 99.9% SLA, higher rate limits, and signed point-in-time evidence exports for compliance and audits.
When do the paid tiers launch?
We're validating demand first. Join the waitlist — waitlisters get early access and launch pricing when webhooks ship.
Can I self-host instead?
Yes. The core is MIT-licensed — see the repo. The paid features are the hosted convenience layer.
